As of today, thanks to our geograpgical position and investment in international shipping, and merchantry, Batumi stands as one of the key gateways of the Black Sea and Transcaucasia, connecting Georgia's, and many other countries land-based shipments to sea-based cargo, allowing it access to reach ports and trade centers across both Europe and Asia. Through strong partnerships and modern infrastructure, our routes support international commerce and cooperation. Every vessel that enters or departs from Batumi represents a link in a growing network of trade, logistics, and innovation. As Georgia strengthens its position as a regional hub, Batumi continues to serve as an indispensible bridge between East and West, North and South, uniting all four quadrants under one port.
